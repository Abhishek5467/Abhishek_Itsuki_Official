## Quick orientation

- **Brand:** Abhishek Singh — AI Researcher · Software Engineer · Photonics Researcher @ IIT Patna.
- **Aesthetic:** "Photonic Lab" — deep obsidian surfaces, photon-cyan (`#16EAD5`) signature, optic
  violet / resonance amber / signal red accents, instrument-panel motifs, 3D cursor-tilt flourish.
- **Type:** Space Grotesk (display), Sora (body), JetBrains Mono (data/labels).
- **Entry CSS:** link `styles.css` (imports all tokens + fonts).
- **Components:** `window.AbhishekSinghPortfolioDesignSystem_9edb24.*` — see `readme.md` index and
  each component's `.prompt.md`.
- **Reference screen:** `ui_kits/portfolio/index.html` (full 3D-animated portfolio).
- **Assets:** `assets/` (portrait, résumé, Sam Animation logo, research heatmaps). Brand/social icons
  via simple-icons CDN. Avoid emoji in rendered UI.
