Headline metric tile for ranks, ratings and benchmark scores.

```jsx
<StatCard label="GATE ECE" value="1046" unit="AIR" sub="Score 662" />
<StatCard label="DA-HPFS · CUHK" value="0.816" unit="AUC" tone="optic" sub="+2.0% over baseline" />
<StatCard label="DSA Solved" value="1000" unit="+" tone="lime" />
```

`tone` tints the corner glow and unit. Use tabular figures — great in a responsive grid of 3–4.
