Signature 3D flourish — a surface that tilts toward the cursor with a photon glare and parallax content.

```jsx
<TiltCard intensity={10} style={{ padding: "2rem", maxWidth: 380 }}>
  <Avatar src="assets/abhishek-portrait.png" size={72} ring />
  <h3>Abhishek Singh</h3>
  <p>AI &amp; Photonics Researcher</p>
</TiltCard>
```

Use for the hero portrait card or a featured project. `intensity` sets max tilt degrees. Honors reduced-motion (tilt disabled).
